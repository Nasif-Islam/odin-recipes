Lasagna recipe

Description

A classic lasagna recipe involves layering wide, flat pasta sheets with a hearty meat (ragù) and/or a creamy white (béchamel) sauce, a cheese mixture (typically ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and then baking the casserole until it's golden, bubbly, and cooked through. The final result is a rich and comforting baked Italian dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Marinara or Meat Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Egg (optional but recommended)
  • Seasonings (optional but recommended)

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Prepare the cheese sauce
  4. Preheat oven
  5. Assemble the lasagna
  6. Bake and rest
